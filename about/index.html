Jizelle is the creative force behind Vagabond Studio. Learning that drawing or painting wasn’t her medium, Jizelle found a a perfect outlet for her bridled creativity through digital design and development. She loves to create crisp, clean, code which is functional, accessible and imaginative.


Jizelle is an avid traveler who spent the last couple of years working abroad in London as a teacher before living and exploring by campervan. When she isn’t coding or wandering, she can be found in nature or hanging at the beach with her boxer, Bali.
